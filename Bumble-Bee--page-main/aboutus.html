About us

Welcome to the Bumblebee Foundation, established in 2020 as a Non-Profit Company with a clear mission:

to serve as a conduit for both local and offshore sponsors seeking to make a meaningful impact within South Africa.

. Our primary focus lies in fostering partnerships with offshore sponsors, leveraging their resources to support the initiatives of other non profit organisations.

 

Our goals

Vision Statement

At Bumblebee Foundation, we envision a place where every individual and community has the opportunity to thrive. We strive for a future where social and environmental challenges are met with empathy, innovation, and collaboration. Our vision is one of interconnectedness, where offshore sponsors and local initiatives unite to create sustainable solutions that leave a lasting impact. Through our dedication to transparency, accountability, and inclusivity, we aspire to be a beacon of hope and catalyst for positive change worldwide. Together, we can build a future where everyone has the resources and support they need to reach their full potential.

Mission Statement

The Bumblebee Foundation is dedicated to fostering partnerships and mobilising resources to support the initiatives of non-profit organisations within South Africa. Our mission is to connect offshore sponsors with local projects, amplifying their impact and empowering communities to address pressing social and environmental challenges. Committed to transparency, accountability, and sustainability, we strive to maximize the effectiveness of every donation and ensure that resources are directed towards meaningful, long-term solutions. Through collaboration and collective action, we aim to create a more equitable and resilient space for present and future generations.

Daily Statement

At the heart of our foundation is the belief in the power of collaboration and collective action. We understand that real change happens when individuals and organisations come together to address pressing social and environmental challenges. By harnessing the generosity of offshore sponsors and facilitating their support for local initiatives, we strive to create a ripple effect of positive change that extends far beyond geographical boundaries. We strive to work towards a brighter, more equitable future for all less fortunate and in despondence poverty. Together, we can create lasting change and leave a legacy of compassion and generosity for generations to come.
